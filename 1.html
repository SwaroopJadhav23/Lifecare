    • Neuropsychological Services Jehangir Centre for Neuropsychology follows internationally recommended holistic neuropsychological rehabilitation model for providing rehabilitation to those suffering from different brain injury conditions. The centre specializes in head trauma, stroke and dementia management. It is the first and the only centre in the region to provide comprehensive neuropsychological rehabilitation. Unique features of the service include:

    • Neuropsychological (Cognitive) assessment
    • Brain injury education
    • Memory/ cognitive rehabilitation
    • Assistive technology based cognitive rehabilitation
    • Addressing issues related to return to work/studies after a brain injury
    • Interventions for behavioural & emotional problems
    • Psychotherapy and counselling for patients and families
    • Availability of brain injury related education materia
    • Follows multi-disciplinary approach
